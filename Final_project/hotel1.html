Located in San Diego and with San Diego Convention Center reachable within a 10-minute walk, Pendry San Diego has an outdoor swimming pool, non-smoking rooms, free WiFi and a fitness center. Located in the Downtown San Diego district, the property provides guests with a restaurant. The property is half a mile from the city Downtown San Diego and 1.3 miles from USS Midway Museum.

All guest rooms at the hotel are equipped with a seating area. The rooms are equipped with a private bathroom with a hair dryer. At Pendry San Diego every room includes a desk and a flat-screen TV.

An American breakfast is served each morning at the property.

Staff at the reception can help guests with any questions that they might have.
